Elite Ships

The list below contains all (38)* currently available ships ingame that range from the iconic Sidewinder, various small combat and multipurpose vessels up to heavy class cruisers like the Imperial Cutter or the Federal Corvette.

Even though each ship can be fitted in numerous ways to fulfil your need of whatever role you take part, some of them excel at different roles more than the other - so take your pick and fly safe.

*CQC ships like the Imperial fighter and the F63 Condor are not included.
